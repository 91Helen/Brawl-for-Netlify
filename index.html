What is Brawl Stars?

Brawl Stars is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Supercell. Players can choose "Brawlers" with distinct abilities, and play in different game modes such as Gem Grab, Showdown, Brawl Ball.

Brawl Stars' Game Modes

3v3 Events/5v5 Events


  • Gem Grab: Teams collect and hold gems that spawn in the middle of the map.
  • Brawl Ball: Teams try to score goals with a ball found at the center of the map.
  • Heist: Teams try to crack open each other’s safe while defending their own.
  • Bounty: Players collect stars. The team with the most stars at the end wins.

Special Events

  • Special modes that often provide special rewards:

  • Robo Rumble: Players defend a safe against waves of robots.
  • Boss Fight:Players team up to defeat a big boss robot.
